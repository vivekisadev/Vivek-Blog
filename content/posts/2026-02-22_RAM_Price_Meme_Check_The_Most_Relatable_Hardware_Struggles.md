# RAM Price Meme Check: The Most Relatable Hardware Struggles

The RAM market is still rough. The best way to cope is dark humor. Here's this week's hardware meme check.

## The Developer RAM Experience in 2026

### Scenario 1: Building a New PC

```
Budget: $1,500
GPU: $600 ✅
CPU: $350 ✅
Motherboard: $200 ✅
RAM (32GB DDR5): $155
Remaining for storage, PSU, case: $195

*Proceeds to buy 16GB instead and regrets it immediately*
```

### Scenario 2: The Daily Development Experience

```
9:00 AM

Total: 11.3GB / 16GB

• Opens Figma

• Closes Slack

```

### Scenario 5: The Realisation

> DDR4 32GB: $60 (used)
> DDR5 32GB: $155 (new)
> 
> The platform that accepts DDR4: compatible with my CPU

## The Real Talk

It's cyclical. RAM always goes up, then crashes. The DDR6 transition will eventually crater DDR5 prices. Patience is the play.

Or just close some Chrome tabs. That's free.

## Current Price Watch

Prices as of Feb 2026:
- 32GB DDR5-6000: ~$145-165 depending on brand/speed
- 32GB DDR4-3600: ~$42-55 (incredible value)
- 64GB DDR5 Kit: ~$260-290

The DDR4 deal is remarkable right now. If your platform accepts it, strongly consider.
