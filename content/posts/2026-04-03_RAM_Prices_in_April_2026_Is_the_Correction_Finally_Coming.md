# RAM Prices in April 2026: Is the Correction Finally Coming?

We have been tracking RAM prices since they started climbing in early 2025. April 2026 brings some interesting signals.

## Current Price Snapshot April 2026

| Type | Price | vs Jan 2026 |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 16GB DDR5-5600 | ~$84 | -5% |
| 32GB DDR5-6000 | ~$148 | -8% |
| 64GB DDR5 Kit | ~$252 | -6% |
| 32GB DDR4-3600 | ~$44 | flat |

A slight softening. Not a crash — but the first consistent downward movement since mid-2024.

## What Is Driving the Softening

Samsung fab capacity is coming online. The new DRAM fab in Pyeongtaek began meaningful production in Q1 2026. Supply is incrementally increasing.

PC market has stabilized. After the AI-PC upgrade cycle, inventory build is happening at OEMs. Demand is more predictable.

Smartphone LPDDR5X restocking is complete. The mobile demand surge that consumed 2025 fab capacity has largely normalized.

## What Has Not Changed

AI data center demand remains ferocious. SK Hynix is capacity-constrained on HBM3E through at least Q3 2026. This keeps a floor under overall DRAM pricing.

## The Buying Guide Right Now
Immediate need on DDR5 platform:   Buy now. Softening not crashing.
Can wait 3-6 months:               Q3-Q4 2026 likely meaningfully better.
On DDR4 platform:                  Used DDR4 is exceptional value at $44 for 32GB.
Building new system:               DDR5. DDR6 platforms are not mainstream yet.

## The India Situation

Import duties plus distributor margins mean Indian prices lag global corrections by 4-6 weeks. If global prices soften in April, expect Indian retail to reflect it by May-June. Watch Vedant Computers, MD Computers, and Primeabgb for price movements.
