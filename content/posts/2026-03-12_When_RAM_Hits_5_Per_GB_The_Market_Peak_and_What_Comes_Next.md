# When RAM Hits $5 Per GB: The Market Peak and What Comes Next

DDR5 pricing has pushed toward the $5/GB mark in some configurations. Let's analyze whether we're at peak pricing and what signals to watch.

## The Price Per GB Analysis

```
DDR5-5600 32GB Kit:   $150 → $4.69/GB
DDR5-6000 32GB Kit:   $162 → $5.06/GB
DDR5-6400 32GB Kit:   $178 → $5.56/GB

Compare to DDR4 peak (2018-2019):
DDR4-3200 16GB Kit:   $85  → $5.31/GB

So we're approximately at DDR4 peak pricing for DDR5. Historically, this precedes a correction.
```

## Historical DRAM Price Cycles

The DRAM market operates in clear boom-bust cycles:

```
2016-2018: Supply shortage → Prices triple
2019-2020: Oversupply → Prices crash 50%
2021-2022: Pandemic demand → Prices spike
2022-2023: Inventory glut → Prices crash 60%
2023-2025: AI demand + retooling → Prices rise 60%+
2026-2027: New capacity + DDR6 → Prices should normalize
```

## The Market Signals to Watch

**Bearish for prices (good for buyers):**
- Samsung DRAM capex announcements (more fab investment = more future supply)
- DDR6 OEM system launches (signals platform transition, DDR5 demand shifts)
- Chinese DRAM manufacturers (CXMT) gaining market share
- Global PC shipment slowdowns (less DDR5 demand)

**Bullish for prices (bad for buyers):**
- New AI accelerator generations requiring more memory bandwidth
- Government AI infrastructure spending
- Earthquake/disaster affecting Taiwan/South Korea fabs
- Continued smartphone memory demand growth

## The Developer's Investment Thesis

If you believe prices will come down (and history suggests they will):

```
Option A: Buy now at peak → Pay full price, get productivity today
Option B: Wait 6 months → Risk prices going higher (unlikely) or save 20-30%
Option C: Buy DDR4 platform used → Excellent value, skip the peak entirely
```

For a developer workstation: Option A is often the right call. Productivity loss from RAM constraints costs more than the RAM price premium over 6-12 months.

## DRAM Stocks as a Barometer

MU (Micron) stock movement often predicts DRAM price direction 3-6 months early. When analysts anticipate price recovery, MU rallies. Monitor it as a signal even if you don't invest.
