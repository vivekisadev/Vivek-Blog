# A Valentine to the Code That Made Us Who We Are

Happy Valentine's Day. In the spirit of love, let's celebrate the code, languages, and technologies that shaped us as developers.

## Love Letters to Our Tools

### To CSS

*Dear CSS,*
You've frustrated me more than any relationship I've ever been in. You never do what I ask. `margin: auto` works. Until it doesn't. `z-index: 9999` seems infinite until something goes above it.

But you've taught me patience. You've shown me that things that look simple are often complex. And when I finally get that layout right after two hours of struggle — it's the most satisfying feeling in programming.

I wouldn't change a thing.

*Except the box model. That needed to change.*

### To Stack Overflow

*Dear Stack Overflow,*
You were there for me at 2am when nobody else was. You have the answer to every question I've ever had, buried somewhere in 2009 with syntax highlighting that hasn't aged well.

I have 1,247 reputation points. I am deeply proud of this.

### To Git

*Dear Git,*
I don't understand you. I've never fully understood you. Every time I think I do, I accidentally `git reset --hard` something important.

But you've saved me more times than you've hurt me. Probably. The reflog is a safety net I've needed more than I'd like to admit.

### To npm

*Dear npm,*
`node_modules` is the bloated mess of our relationship. But look at what we've built together.

I'm switching to Bun but I'll always have feelings for you.
